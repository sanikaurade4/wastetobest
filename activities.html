Our Activities

Join us in our efforts to promote recycling, creativity, and sustainability through these engaging and impactful activities!

Workshops

Join our workshops and learn how to turn waste into beautiful and functional items. From upcycled fashion to home décor, get creative with us!

Community Clean-Up

Help us clean up local parks, rivers, and neighborhoods while learning about recycling and proper waste management!

Recycling Challenge

Take part in exciting challenges where you create innovative products from recycled materials. Prizes await the most creative ideas!

Awareness Campaign

Join us for social media challenges, poster-making contests, and other campaigns that educate others on the importance of recycling.
